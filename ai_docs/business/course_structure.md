# Course Structure

## Course Architecture Overview

**Two-Tier Structure:**
1. **Free Mini-Course:** 12 foundational lessons (trust-building, lead generation)

**Learning Path:** Free → Paid → Implementation → Community Support

## Paid Full Course (5 Modules, ~8 Hours)

### Module 1: Platform Economics & Profitability Calculation
**Duration:** 2 hours
**Access:** All paid tiers (Basic, Accelerator, VIP)

**Structure:**
- 6 video lessons (15-20 min each)
- Excel profitability calculator (template)
- Commission scenarios worksheet
- Breakeven analysis tool

**Content:**

**1.1 - The Real Commission Structure (25 min)**
- Base commission rates: 20-25% (GoFood) vs. 25-30% (GrabFood)
- Hidden fees: payment processing, delivery, service
- Promotional discounts: who pays what
- VAT and tax implications for foreign owners
- Total effective commission: often 30-36% all-in

**1.2 - True Profit Calculation (20 min)**
- COGS for delivery vs. dine-in (packaging, portions)
- Labor allocation: kitchen vs. delivery prep
- Overhead: rent, utilities, platform fees
- **The danger zone:** Items that lose money after commission
- Profit calculator walkthrough with real examples

**1.3 - Menu Pricing for Profitability (20 min)**
- Reverse engineering: starting from target margin
- Pricing tiers: economy, mid-range, premium within one menu
- Delivery-only items: higher margins to offset commission
- Bundling math: how combos improve profitability
- Dynamic pricing: surge vs. slow hours (when legal)

**1.4 - Financial Forecasting (20 min)**
- Order volume projections by season
- Revenue modeling: scenarios (pessimistic, realistic, optimistic)
- Cash flow management: dealing with platform payment delays
- Scaling economics: when to add kitchen capacity
- Breakeven analysis: how many orders to cover costs

**1.5 - Cost Control Strategies (15 min)**
- Negotiating better commission rates (volume discounts)
- Reducing packaging costs without looking cheap
- Minimizing food waste in delivery operations
- Labor efficiency: batching, prep optimization
- Technology: POS integration to reduce errors

**1.6 - Case Study: Profitability Turnaround (15 min)**
- Real Bali restaurant losing money on delivery
- Audit findings: 40% of menu items unprofitable
- Solution: Menu pruning, repricing, bundling
- Results: 15% overall profit margin achieved in 3 months

**Deliverables:**
- Profitability calculator (Excel/Google Sheets)
- Menu pricing template
- Breakeven analysis spreadsheet
- Commission negotiation email template

**Homework:**
- Calculate true profit margin on current menu
- Identify unprofitable items to remove or reprice
- Set profitability targets for next quarter

### Module 2: Advertising & ROI Framework
**Duration:** 2 hours
**Access:** All paid tiers

**Structure:**
- 6 video lessons (15-20 min each)
- Ad budget calculator (template)
- Campaign tracking spreadsheet
- A/B testing framework

**Content:**

**2.1 - Ad Types Deep Dive (25 min)**
- **CPO (Cost Per Order):** Auction dynamics, bidding strategies, when it works
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Awareness campaigns, CTR optimization, pitfalls
- **CPM (Cost Per Mille):** Branding plays, limited use cases
- **Featured Placement:** Homepage vs. category placement, costs, ROI
- **Promotional Stakes:** Platform-funded discounts, qualification, application process

**2.2 - Budget Setting & Allocation (20 min)**
- Starting point: 5-10% of delivery revenue
- Test budget: $10-20/day for initial campaigns
- Scaling rules: when to increase, when to pause
- Seasonal adjustments: high vs. low season budgets
- Platform split: GoFood vs. GrabFood budget allocation

**2.3 - ROI Calculation & Tracking (20 min)**
- **True cost per order:** Ad spend + commission + fees
- **Customer lifetime value:** Repeat rate, average frequency
- **Payback period:** How long until ads pay for themselves
- **Attribution:** Direct ads vs. spillover organic traffic
- **Tools:** Tracking pixels, dashboard analytics, manual logs

**2.4 - Campaign Optimization (20 min)**
- **A/B testing:** Creative, targeting, bidding, timing
- **Time-of-day optimization:** When to run ads, when to pause
- **Geographic targeting:** Radius refinement, hotspot focus
- **Audience targeting:** New customers vs. lapsed vs. lookalike
- **Creative best practices:** Images, copy, offers that convert

**2.5 - Common Mistakes & How to Avoid (15 min)**
- **Mistake #1:** Setting budgets too high too fast (burn rate)
- **Mistake #2:** Not tracking ROI (flying blind)
- **Mistake #3:** Wrong ad type for goal (CPC for sales)
- **Mistake #4:** Ignoring organic visibility (ads as crutch)
- **Mistake #5:** Listening to account manager's high-spend advice
- **Prevention:** Frameworks, checklists, tracking discipline

**2.6 - Case Study: $50/day to $500/day Profitably (15 min)**
- Café starting with small ad budget
- Testing protocol: CPO vs. CPC, different times, creatives
- Winning formula identified: CPO, 11am-1pm, food close-ups
- Scaling process: $10 → $50 → $200 → $500/day
- Results: 300% order volume increase, 2.5x ROI maintained

**Deliverables:**
- Ad budget calculator with ROI projections
- Campaign tracking spreadsheet (daily/weekly reporting)
- A/B testing framework template
- Creative brief template for designers

**Homework:**
- Set up tracking system for current ad campaigns
- Calculate current ROI (may be negative - that's okay)
- Design one A/B test to run next week

### Module 4: Operational Excellence
**Duration:** 1.5 hours
**Access:** All paid tiers

**Structure:**
- 5 video lessons (15-20 min each)
- SOP templates (kitchen, front-of-house)
- Incident response playbook
- Quality control checklist

**Content:**

**4.1 - OTP Compliance Deep Dive (20 min)**
- **OTP rules explained:** Accept times, preparation times, handoff protocols
- **Common violations:** Late acceptance, wrong prep times, incomplete orders
- **Penalties:** Warnings, visibility drops, account suspension
- **Prevention:** Systems, training, technology (auto-accept, timers)
- **Recovery:** What to do if you violate OTP

**4.2 - Kitchen Workflow for Delivery (18 min)**
- **Station design:** Dedicated delivery prep area vs. shared
- **Order batching:** Efficiency without sacrificing quality
- **Packaging standards:** Temperature retention, leak prevention, presentation
- **Quality control:** Final checks before handoff to driver
- **Speed without shortcuts:** Maintaining standards under pressure

**4.3 - Cancellation Prevention (18 min)**
- **Why cancellations hurt:** Ranking penalties, revenue loss, customer trust
- **Common causes:** Out-of-stock, too busy, order mistakes
- **Prevention strategies:**
  - Real-time inventory management (stop-lists)
  - Capacity planning (don't accept more than you can handle)
  - Clear communication with drivers
- **When cancellation is necessary:** How to minimize damage

**4.4 - Stop-List Management (15 min)**
- **What is stop-list:** Temporarily disabling menu items
- **When to use:** Out-of-stock, supplier delays, equipment failure
- **How to update:** Platform interfaces, timing, communication
- **Mistakes to avoid:** Forgetting to re-enable, overusing as crutch
- **Alternative:** Dynamic menu (smaller during busy times)

**4.5 - Team Training & SOPs (15 min)**
- **Who needs training:** Kitchen, cashier, manager, owner
- **Training modules:** Platform mechanics, OTP rules, quality standards
- **SOP templates:** Step-by-step processes for common scenarios
- **Quality circles:** Regular team reviews of delivery performance
- **Incentives:** Tying bonuses to delivery metrics (ratings, speed)
